Our Approach

How We Work

  1. 01

    Site Assessment

    Review geotechnical data. Evaluate soil conditions and loads.

  2. 02

    Engineering Design

    Dr. Garbin designs column layout, spacing, and depths.

  3. 03

    Mobilization

    Our fleet deploys from Fort Wayne. On-site within days.

  4. 04

    Installation

    Vibroflot drives to depth. Stone compacted in lifts. 40-60/day.

  5. 05

    Quality Verification

    Real-time data logging. Modulus load testing on every project.

  6. 06

    Closeout

    As-built documentation. QC reports. Ongoing engineer support.
