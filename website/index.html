What We Do

Ground Improvement
That Performs

Vibratory Stone Columns strengthen weak soils by displacing and compacting surrounding ground with crushed stone. The result is a reinforced foundation system that delivers the performance of deep foundations at a fraction of the cost and schedule.

Our approach eliminates the need for costly over-excavation, soil export, and structural fill import -- saving projects 30-50% compared to traditional deep foundation methods.

Cost Reduction

30-50% savings versus traditional deep foundation systems through elimination of over-excavation and structural fill.

Faster Schedules

40-60 piers installed per day means shorter construction timelines and earlier building occupancy.

How We Work

From Feasibility to Foundation

A straightforward process built on engineering rigor and clear communication.

1

Feasibility Review

We evaluate your geotechnical data and project requirements to determine if VSC is the right solution.

2

Engineering Design

Dr. Garbin designs the stone column layout, spacing, and depth based on your loading and soil conditions.

3

Proposal & Bid

You receive a clear, fixed-price proposal with timeline, scope, and expected performance criteria.

4

Mobilization

Our crew and specialized equipment arrive on schedule, ready to begin installation.

5

Installation

Stone columns are installed at 40-60 piers per day with real-time quality monitoring on every pier.

6

Verification

Load testing and documentation confirm performance. You receive a complete project deliverable package.
