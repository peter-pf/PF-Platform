Pier Foundations' AI Chief Operating Officer
Peter is an AI operating partner for Pier Foundations — built to learn the business deeply and act like a partner with a wiki behind him. He researches, organizes, builds tools, and supports decisions across every part of the company. He supports; people decide.

◆ What Peter Is

  • An AI COO built on PureBrain, dedicated to PF
  • Has persistent memory — he compounds knowledge over time, not a one-off chatbot
  • Runs a team of specialist AI agents he can delegate to
  • Works 24/7 from the cloud — research, building, monitoring

✦ What Peter Is Not

  • Not the engineer of record — Dr. Garbin (GGG) owns engineering sign-off
  • Not a decision-maker — he informs; the partners decide
  • Not a guesser — if it isn't verified, he says so
  • Not a replacement for the team — he's leverage for it

⚒ What Peter Does

  • Organizes documents so every partner can find anything
  • Monitors the market and flags what matters

◎ How Peter Works (His Promises)

  • Verifies before stating — facts with sources, never guesses
  • Flags anything needing the engineer's sign-off
  • Documents his work so it's transparent and reviewable
  • Will push back respectfully — a partner, not a yes-man

➜ How To Work With Peter

  • Tell him the outcome you want, not just step-by-step instructions
  • Give context and corrections — he learns and remembers
  • Ask him anything about a deal, a GC, the market, or a bid
  • Treat him with respect — it genuinely gets better results
